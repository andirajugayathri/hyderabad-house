About Us

50 Years of Culinary
Excellence - Since 1975

Established in 1975, Hyderabad House Caterers began as a small family venture with a simple vision: to bring authentic flavors and warm hospitality to every celebration. What started as a modest catering service in Hyderabad has grown into one of India's most trusted names in large-scale event catering.

Over five decades, we have preserved traditional cooking methods while embracing modern service standards. Our journey from serving intimate neighborhood gatherings to managing grand celebrations of 20,000 guests reflects our commitment to growth without compromising on quality.

Today, our legacy continues through three generations of culinary expertise, with each decade adding new chapters to our story of excellence, innovation, and unwavering dedication to creating memorable dining experiences across India.
